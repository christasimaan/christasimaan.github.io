Keeping up with Christa!

The inside scoop, the rundown, the 411, etc!

Typically, I can be found doing any of the following: golfing, cooking, talking, watching football (Go Steelers!), traveling, advocating for women in STEM, listening to music, admiring any and every form of body of water, hiking, solving the daily mini, and/or gardening.

I would say 99% of the time, there is music coming out of my computer! Music can instantly change my mood (always for the better :))

I also adore meeting new people. I happen to inherit the traits of being boisterous and gregarious, which I attribute to my Lebanese heritage. My whole life I have been told that I have never met a stranger, that I am the person that knows everyone. For as long as I can remember, the only struggle I ever experienced at school was when to stop talking. I get joy from being around people. I am an extreme extrovert who gets energized from having conversations.

Lastly, it is mainly the little things in life that I truly value and enjoy the most.

A day in the life really tends to vary. However, when I am somewhere, I am fully there. When I am at school, I typically tend to go to classes, then head to the library, meet someone for a quick dinner, and then head back to the library! While this may seem very anticlimactic, I enjoy it as I know I need to be fully present and focus on my studies.

During the summer, after work, I typically explore the city, see some friends, listen to music, work-out, and then repeat!

I always like to be doing something, so free-time does not come very often haha. When it does, I typically will just listen to music, code, or do logic puzzles; I am not a big TV or movie fan.
